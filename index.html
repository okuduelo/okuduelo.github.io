ENG

OKU is a space that supports the grieving process. Its visual identity, inspired by the constructivist theory of grief, conceives it as a flexible and ever-evolving journey, oscillating between emptiness and reconstruction. The logo expands to create voids that hold text and images, a metaphor for how life is rebuilt after loss. The project unfolds across an editorial piece, a web prototype, and graphic applications, shaping a dynamic and sensitive visual language that seeks to represent the emotions of those in mourning.
